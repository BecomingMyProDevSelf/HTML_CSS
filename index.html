Welcome

Thank you for visiting Orion Astrology. We calculate our predictions with movement of planets inside a period of time and Predictions are based on daily charts. Each sign is placed in a specific house in the charter. Then the position of each planet is calculated and the planet is placed in the proper home. Then, the astrologer can predict the impact of the movement of the planet on each sign. Personalized reading based on the charter of each person would be more specific, but daily horoscopes give a good general indication. The weekly and monthly preachingare also calculated in the same way.
